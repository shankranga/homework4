Crystal Collector!

This is how you play Crystal Collector


  1. A random number will be given to you to begin the game
  2. There are four crystals that are here below
    • By Clicking a crystal you will add a certain amount of points to the total score
    • You win if your total score matches the score given
    • The values of the crystal will be hidden until you click on a crystal
    • The values of the Crystals will change when the game is reset
